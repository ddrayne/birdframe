# gongbi-garden-scroll

## Collection
Print Traditions

## Description
A patient garden portrait built from precise brush contours, translucent colour, and a spacious relationship between every living thing.

## Lineage
Chinese gongbi bird-and-flower painting traditions

## Medium
ink and mineral colour on prepared silk

## Palette
tea green, cinnabar, shell white, ink, pale mineral blue

## Affinities
species-rich, even-chorus, spring, summer, clear, all-day

## Prompt
A meticulous Chinese gongbi bird-and-flower hanging scroll depicting {scene}. Use disciplined fine-line brush contours, layered translucent mineral colour, botanically specific Edinburgh garden plants, accurate plumage and generous breathing space on prepared silk. Compose distinct species in a graceful vertical journey, with temporal activity expressed through flowering rhythm and spatial intervals rather than repeated birds.

## Avoid
loose splash ink, photographic realism, generic fantasy blossoms, duplicated birds, calligraphy, seals, watermarks
