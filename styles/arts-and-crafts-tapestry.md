# arts-and-crafts-tapestry

## Collection
Decorative Histories

## Description
Birds and local plants woven into one democratic surface, with every leaf and weather mark belonging to the day.

## Lineage
British Arts and Crafts textiles, tapestries and nature patterns of the late nineteenth century

## Medium
handwoven wool tapestry with naturally dyed yarn

## Palette
madder, weld, indigo, walnut, faded moss

## Affinities
species-rich, even-chorus, autumn, summer, rain, all-day

## Prompt
A British Arts and Crafts handwoven tapestry depicting {scene}. Interlock recognisable birds with Edinburgh garden leaves, berries, seed heads and weather motifs across a shallow continuous field. Visible wool warp and weft, naturally dyed colour, rhythmic repeats with meaningful variation, strong silhouette and no illusionistic depth. Portrait hanging; detection activity changes pattern density, never the literal number of birds.

## Avoid
seamless computer pattern, synthetic neon, photographic perspective, cloned birds, text, watermarks
