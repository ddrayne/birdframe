# dawn-chorus-clock

## Collection
Data Portraits

## Description
A circular portrait of the day in which time is structure: voices enter, recede, overlap, and gather around a living clock.

## Lineage
astronomical diagrams, phenological wheels and modern information design

## Medium
screenprinted diagram with hand-painted natural-history vignettes

## Palette
midnight blue through dawn peach to daylight cream, plumage-true accents

## Affinities
dawn-heavy, dusk-heavy, all-day, species-rich, night-active, first-arrival

## Prompt
A beautiful data-led dawn-chorus clock depicting {scene}. Organise the portrait around a subtle 24-hour radial structure: quiet hours remain spacious, active hours gain rings, marks and botanical density, and peak song creates a gentle visual pulse. Place each real species once in a natural relationship to its active part of the clock, accurately painted as a natural-history vignette. No numbers or chart labels. Portrait editorial artwork, emotional before analytical.

## Avoid
dashboard UI, readable numerals, literal bar chart, one bird per detection, duplicated species, infographic icons, watermarks
