# celtic-illuminated-bestiary

## Collection
Decorative Histories

## Description
A jewel-like page where each recorded voice becomes part of an intricate, meditative ecology of knots, margins, and gold.

## Lineage
Insular illuminated manuscripts and medieval bestiary pages

## Medium
mineral pigment, iron-gall ink and raised gold on vellum

## Palette
lapis, verdigris, madder, soot, shell gold, vellum

## Affinities
species-rich, even-chorus, first-arrival, winter, night-active

## Prompt
An Insular illuminated bestiary page depicting {scene}. Render the real birds accurately within a densely interlaced but orderly ecology of knotwork, vine scrolls, minute weather symbols and jewel-like compartments. Mineral pigments, iron-gall outlines, raised gold catches, slightly irregular vellum. Let acoustic richness control ornamental density while each species appears only as needed. Portrait manuscript page.

## Avoid
legible pseudo-Latin text, fantasy creatures, duplicated birds, glossy digital gold, photorealism, watermarks
