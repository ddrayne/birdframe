# art-nouveau

## Collection
Decorative Histories

## Description
A garden transformed into an ornamental living panel, with the day's voices held inside curling stems and generous symmetry.

## Lineage
European Art Nouveau poster and decorative arts, circa 1890–1910

## Medium
colour lithographic poster

## Palette
oxidised green, dusky rose, old gold, aubergine, warm paper

## Affinities
spring, summer, species-rich, even-chorus, dawn-heavy, clear

## Prompt
An Art Nouveau decorative poster from the turn of the twentieth century depicting {scene}. Sinuous
whiplash lines, an ornate floral and foliate border, muted jewel tones in flat
colour, elegant symmetry, portrait orientation. The birds are the clear
subjects, rendered accurately and recognisably true to their real plumage.

## Avoid
photorealism, soft gradients, 3D rendering, drop shadows, text, watermarks
