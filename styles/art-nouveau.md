# art-nouveau

## Prompt
An Art Nouveau decorative poster (Mucha-inspired) depicting {scene}. Sinuous
whiplash lines, an ornate floral and foliate border, muted jewel tones in flat
colour, elegant symmetry, portrait orientation. The birds are the clear
subjects, rendered accurately and recognisably true to their real plumage.

## Avoid
photorealism, soft gradients, 3D rendering, drop shadows, text, watermarks
