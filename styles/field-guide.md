# field-guide

## Prompt
A vintage naturalist's field-guide plate depicting {scene}. Each bird posed
clearly and separately on a warm cream background, fine ink linework with flat
watercolour washes, neat hand-lettered labels feel, in the tradition of a
classic ornithological illustration. Portrait orientation. The birds are the
clear subjects, rendered accurately and recognisably true to their real
plumage.

## Avoid
photorealism, soft gradients, 3D rendering, drop shadows, garish colours, watermarks
