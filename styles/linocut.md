# linocut

## Prompt
A hand-pulled linocut / lino print depicting {scene}. High-contrast carved
marks, one or two ink colours on textured paper, bold negative space, strong
graphic silhouettes. Portrait orientation. The birds are the clear subjects,
rendered accurately and recognisably true to their real shape and markings.

## Avoid
photorealism, gradients, 3D rendering, drop shadows, text, watermarks
