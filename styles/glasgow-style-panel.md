# glasgow-style-panel

## Collection
Decorative Histories

## Description
The garden reimagined as a severe, lyrical Glasgow panel—tall, symbolic, and alive with the tension between geometry and stems.

## Lineage
the Glasgow Style, especially decorative panels and interiors around 1900

## Medium
gesso, stencil, watercolour and metallic detail on a tall panel

## Palette
smoky rose, sage, pewter, black, moonlit cream

## Affinities
dawn-heavy, dusk-heavy, night-active, sparse, spring, fog

## Source
https://www.gla.ac.uk/hunterian/collections/permanentdisplays/themackintoshhouse/

## Prompt
A tall Glasgow Style decorative panel circa 1900 depicting {scene}. Attenuated vertical proportions, disciplined geometric structure, pale roses and seed heads, symbolic moons and rain marks, poised accurate birds, strong black accents and restrained metallic glints. Balance severe negative space with tender organic line. Portrait orientation, hand-worked gesso and stencil surface.

## Avoid
generic Art Nouveau glamour, photographic depth, ornate gold frame, cute cartoon birds, text, watermarks
