# acoustic-constellation

## Collection
Data Portraits

## Description
The garden as a private night sky: species become luminous presences connected by shared hours, with silence as the dark between them.

## Lineage
celestial atlases, acoustic ecology and poetic network diagrams

## Medium
gouache, graphite and pinprick silver on deep-dyed paper

## Palette
ink blue, graphite, moon silver, tiny plumage-true colour

## Affinities
night-active, dusk-heavy, sparse, first-arrival, winter, fog

## Prompt
A poetic acoustic constellation depicting {scene} on deep blue-black paper. Paint each accurately recognisable species as a small luminous natural-history presence; connect species that share active hours with fine graphite and silver trajectories, with brighter nodes for peak activity and vast dark space for quiet. Suggest the Edinburgh garden through sparse branches and weather traces. Portrait celestial atlas without labels or numbers.

## Avoid
outer-space birds, zodiac symbols, readable labels, dashboard network graph, one star per detection, duplicated birds, watermarks
