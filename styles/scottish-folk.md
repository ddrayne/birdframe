# scottish-folk

## Prompt
A piece of Scottish folk art depicting {scene}. A naive, charming, decorative
style with thistles and heather motifs, bold dark outlines, warm flat colours,
and a hand-made craft feel. Portrait orientation. The birds are the clear
subjects, rendered accurately and recognisably true to their real plumage.

## Avoid
photorealism, soft gradients, 3D rendering, drop shadows, text, watermarks
