# soundscape-tapestry

## Collection
Data Portraits

## Description
The acoustic day woven vertically: each species a distinct thread, each hour a change in tension, every silence allowed to breathe.

## Lineage
woven signal notation, modernist textiles and acoustic spectrograms

## Medium
handwoven wool, silk and linen wall hanging

## Palette
weather-led earth colours with bright plumage threads

## Affinities
all-day, even-chorus, species-rich, rain, wind, autumn

## Prompt
A poetic handwoven soundscape tapestry depicting {scene}. The day runs from dark predawn at the top through daylight to night below; each species contributes a distinctive coloured thread and accurate small woven bird portrait, while actual activity density changes knotting, texture and overlap. Silences are broad fields of linen. Tactile fibres, slight handmade irregularity, portrait hanging. Never turn detections into a count of birds.

## Avoid
literal spectrogram software, axes, labels, synthetic gradients, repeated birds, machine-perfect weave, watermarks
