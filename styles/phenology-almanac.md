# phenology-almanac

## Collection
Data Portraits

## Description
A daily almanac page connecting bird voices to season, first arrivals, weather, and the garden's slowly changing year.

## Lineage
naturalists' commonplace books, garden calendars and phenological records

## Medium
pen, watercolour, pressed botanical fragments and letterpress rules

## Palette
seasonal botanical colour, sepia ink, weather blue, warm rag paper

## Affinities
first-arrival, spring, autumn, species-rich, clear, changeable

## Prompt
A richly observed phenology almanac plate depicting {scene}. Combine accurate watercolour birds with seasonal Edinburgh plants, tiny weather notations without words, lunar and daylight motifs, pressed-leaf impressions and delicate ruled compartments. Give first-ever arrivals a quiet ceremonial emphasis. Let detection density influence the fullness of each compartment, never the number of birds. Portrait page from a deeply personal naturalist's lifelong notebook.

## Avoid
readable invented text, calendar numbers, scrapbook kitsch, fake photographs, repeated species, watermarks
