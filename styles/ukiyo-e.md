# ukiyo-e

## Prompt
A traditional Japanese ukiyo-e woodblock print depicting {scene}. Bold flat
areas of colour, confident black outlines, a limited harmonious palette,
visible wood-grain texture, and a calm balanced composition in portrait
orientation. The birds are the clear subjects, rendered accurately and
recognisably true to their real plumage.

## Avoid
photorealism, soft gradients, 3D rendering, drop shadows, text, watermarks
