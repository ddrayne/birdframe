# ukiyo-e

## Collection
Print Traditions

## Description
Weather, branch, bird, and silence held in a poised woodblock composition with the clarity of a single observed moment.

## Lineage
Japanese ukiyo-e and kachō-ga bird-and-flower prints, Edo to Meiji periods

## Medium
multi-block colour woodcut on washi

## Palette
indigo, persimmon, tea green, sumi, unprinted paper

## Affinities
sparse, dominant-species, rain, snow, wind, spring, dusk-heavy

## Prompt
A traditional Japanese ukiyo-e woodblock print depicting {scene}. Bold flat
areas of colour, confident black outlines, a limited harmonious palette,
visible wood-grain texture, and a calm balanced composition in portrait
orientation. The birds are the clear subjects, rendered accurately and
recognisably true to their real plumage.

## Avoid
photorealism, soft gradients, 3D rendering, drop shadows, text, watermarks
