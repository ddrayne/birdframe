# childrens-book

## Prompt
A warm children's picture-book illustration depicting {scene}. Gentle rounded
shapes, a cosy inviting palette, soft flat colour with a hand-painted
storybook charm. Portrait orientation. The birds are the clear subjects,
rendered accurately and recognisably true to their real plumage.

## Avoid
photorealism, harsh gradients, 3D rendering, drop shadows, text, watermarks
