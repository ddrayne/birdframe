# mughal-natural-history

## Collection
Global Natural Histories

## Description
An exquisitely observed album page that treats each garden visitor as singular, worthy, and jewel-like.

## Lineage
Mughal natural-history album painting of the seventeenth century

## Medium
opaque watercolour and gold on burnished paper

## Palette
mineral blue, malachite, vermilion, shell gold, buff paper

## Affinities
dominant-species, first-arrival, sparse, clear, spring, summer

## Prompt
A seventeenth-century Mughal natural-history album page depicting {scene}. Paint the birds with exceptionally precise observation, fine opaque watercolour modelling and minute feather detail, standing or perching in a sparse ground of accurately rendered Edinburgh plants. Surround the scene with a restrained marbled or floral border and tiny gold accents. Dignified frontal clarity, burnished paper, portrait album format.

## Avoid
invented hybrid species, imperial human figures, legible inscriptions, photographic background, excessive ornament over birds, watermarks
