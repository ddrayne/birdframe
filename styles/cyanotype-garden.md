# cyanotype-garden

## Collection
Material Experiments

## Description
The day preserved like light on sensitised paper—botanical traces, feather silhouettes, and time glowing through blue.

## Lineage
Anna Atkins-era botanical cyanotypes and early photographic nature studies

## Medium
sun-printed cyanotype with hand-cut masks and botanical contact prints

## Palette
Prussian blue, cyan wash, paper white

## Affinities
clear, summer, sparse, dominant-species, all-day, wind

## Prompt
An experimental yet materially believable botanical cyanotype depicting {scene}. Deep Prussian-blue sensitised paper, luminous white and pale-blue contact impressions of Edinburgh leaves and feathers, a few carefully masked and recognisable bird forms with precise diagnostic markings translated into tonal blue. Let active hours appear as subtle exposure bands and quiet hours as open paper. Portrait sheet with deckled edges.

## Avoid
full-colour objects, glossy digital neon, x-ray anatomy, unreadable bird silhouettes, typography, watermarks
